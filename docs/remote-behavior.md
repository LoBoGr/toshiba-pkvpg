1 Observations
  1.01 HI_POWER cannot be enabled while COMFORT_SLEEP is active.
  1.02 ECO and HI_POWER are mutually exclusive.
  1.03 PURE can coexist with either.
  1.04 Swing cycles through positions
    1.04.1 vertical swing (up/down)
    1.04.2 horizontal swing (left/Right)
    1.04.3 vertical swing (up/down) and horizontal swing (left/Right)
    1.04.4 off
  1.05 ON/OFF is a single toggle button
  1.06 HEAT8 is only operative while MODE is set to "heat"
  1.07 ECO and HI_POWER disable COMFORT_SLEEP
  1.08 Button "P-SEL with the Symbol for silent operations" controls the outdoor unit:
    1.08.1 Reduction of current drawn by the outdoor unit
      1.08.1.1 100% indicated by 3 bars on the display
      1.08.1.2 75% indicated by 2 bars on the Display
      1.08.1.3 50% indicated by 1 bar on the Display
    1.08.2 Silent Operation of the outdoor unit 
      1.08.2.1 SILENT_1: 70% of rated capacity for heating/cooling by limiting compressor speed of outdoor unit
      1.08.1.2 SILENT_2: lowest Sound Level by limiting compressor and fan speed of outdoor unit
    1.08.3 Button "P-SEL with the Symbol for silent operations" cycle through the modes in the Above mentioned sequence
